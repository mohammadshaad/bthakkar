About Dr. Bharat Thakkar

Professor Thakkar is a widely respected Reliability Engineering and Management expert with decades of grounding in mechanical engineering. He brings with him high-end fusion of engineering and management having worked at major corporations.

After his retirement from his corporate life, he is a much sought after professor at several Chicago institutions, including the Illinois Institute of Technology, the North Illinois University and the Walden University. During a career spanning nearly five decades he has published several papers and overseen many projects to a successful fruition. Apart from his professional calling, Dr. Thakkar is a high regarded poet in the Gujarati and English languages with several collections to his name.

Reliability Engineering & Management

Dr. Thakkar has been offering workshops worldwide on 'Reliability Engineering & Management' for the past over 20 years. At the heart of the workshops is the belief that all products require reliability. "Reliability is what brings you profit," Dr Thakkar says, "Without reliability it is like sailing in the ocean without any direction."
